More Than Just a Fete

Connecting Our Community in Surry Hills

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the fete opening hours?

The fete will be open from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM on Saturday, 13th of September 2025.

Is there parking available?

There is limited street parking available around Surry Hills. We highly recommend using public transport. Central Station is a short walk away.

What payment methods are accepted?

The fete is a ticket voucher system. Tickets can be purchased on the day with card or cash. Stalls will also accept cash if correct amount is offered.

Is the event pet-friendly?

While we love our furry friends, for the safety and comfort of all guests, only service animals are permitted on the grounds.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, the main areas of the fete are wheelchair accessible. Please see our Visit Us Page for wheelchair access detailed information.
