Getting Here

Find the best way to get to Chinese Presbyterian Church, 388-394 Crown St, Surry Hills NSW 2010.

By Bus

The Chinese Presbyterian Church is well-served by several bus routes. The closest bus stops are located on Cleveland Street and Crown Street.

From Central Railway Station: Use bus route numbers 391, 376, 374, or 339.

  • Cleveland Street: Routes 304, 305, 308, 309, 310, 311, 313, 314, 316, 317. These stops are a short walk (approximately 5-7 minutes) to the church.
  • Crown Street: Routes 304, 305. These stops are very close to the church entrance.

Check the Transport NSW website or use a journey planner for real-time bus information from your location.

By Train & Light Rail

The nearest train stations are Central Station and Museum Station, both within walking distance of the church.

  • Central Station: Approximately a 15-20 minute walk to the church. All Sydney train lines connect here, making it a convenient option from various parts of Sydney.
  • Museum Station: Approximately a 10-12 minute walk to the church.

By Light Rail: From Town Hall, take the L2 tram towards Randwick to the Surry Hills stop (before Riley Street). Walk approximately 10 minutes to the church.

By Car & Parking

Street parking is available around the church, however, exact conditions vary street to street. You may find 2-hour and all-day free spots, as well as 4-hour ticket parking. Please be mindful that some streets may also have special event clearways.

Commercial parking stations are also available in the vicinity, such as:

  • Wilson Parking - Goulburn Street (approx. 10-15 min walk)
  • Secure Parking - World Square (approx. 15-20 min walk)

Accessible Access

  • Wheelchair access to the church is via the Albion Street driveway.
  • The Auditorium and Worship Centre have access ramps at the doorways near the courtyard.
  • There are two male and two female ambulant toilets, and a wheelchair accessible toilet available on site.
