Our Story

More Than Just a Fete

The Chinese Presbyterian Church (CPC) is a joyful and multicultural faith community located in the heart of Sydney. Our church family reflects a rich diversity of languages and services — English at 9am and 4pm, Cantonese, Mandarin, and a bilingual service — yet we are united by our shared values and love for fellowship. For us, celebrating culture and connection is deeply woven into our identity. One of our most cherished traditions is the CPC Food Fair and Fellowship Fete. Though our last was in 2018, we’re thrilled to bring it back this year — an event that embodies who we are: a place where food, heritage, and people come together. The Fete is more than just a celebration — it's an open invitation to our neighbours, friends, and families to step through our gates, experience our hospitality, and see what makes CPC unique. From delicious food and fun activities to guided tours and warm conversations, it's a snapshot of our spirit in motion. Whether you’re visiting us for a festival or worship, CPC is a place to belong, grow, and connect.
