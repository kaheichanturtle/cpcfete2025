Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers! Here are some of the most common things people ask about the CPC Fete.

What are the fete opening hours?

The fete will be open from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM on Saturday, 13th of September 2025.

Is there an entry fee?

No, entry to the CPC Fete is completely free! Everyone is welcome to come and enjoy the atmosphere. Individual stalls for food, goods, and some activities may have their own costs.

Where is the fete located?

The fete is held at the Chinese Presbyterian Church, located at 388-394 Crown St, Surry Hills NSW 2010.

What payment methods are accepted?

Most stalls will accept both cash and card (EFTPOS/Credit). However, we recommend bringing some cash for smaller purchases, just in case.

Is there parking available?

There is limited street parking available in the Surry Hills area. We highly recommend using public transport. The fete is a short walk from Central Station and various bus stops.

Is the event pet-friendly?

While we love our furry friends, for the safety and comfort of all our guests in a crowded space, only certified service animals are permitted on the grounds.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, the main ground-level areas of the fete are wheelchair accessible, including access to food stalls and the main stage viewing area. For more detailed information, please see our Visit Us Page.

Are there restrooms available?

Yes, public restrooms, including accessible facilities and a baby-changing station, will be clearly marked on the fete map.
